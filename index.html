Science-Backed

Designed around how your brain actually learns.

Vocable isn't just a word-a-day app. Every feature is built on proven learning science.

Spaced Repetition

Reviewing words at increasing intervals improves long-term retention by up to 200%. Daily notifications and recall challenges are timed to take advantage of this.

Active Recall

Testing yourself on a word's meaning — rather than passively re-reading it — creates stronger neural pathways. Each successful recall makes the memory more durable.

Daily Consistency

Learning just one word per day builds a micro-habit that compounds over time. Brief, repeated encounters with words dramatically improve retention.
